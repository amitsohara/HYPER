# Principle
HyperMind treats thoughts as explicit cognitive objects constructed from the interaction of the World Model, Concepts, Executive Attention, Memory, Goals, and Specialist collaboration.

Thoughts are not final conclusions.
They are evolving cognitive structures that may be refined, challenged, verified, merged, or discarded.

The value of explicit Thought Objects must be established through empirical evaluation rather than assumed.
