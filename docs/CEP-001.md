# Principle
HyperMind does not store intelligence inside predefined concepts.

Concepts emerge naturally from repeated observations, interactions, causal structures, temporal evolution, and accumulated experience represented within the World Model.

Concepts are living cognitive structures.
They may emerge.
They may evolve.
They may merge.
They may split.
They may disappear.

Reasoning operates over concepts.
Learning reshapes concepts.
Experience validates concepts.

The World Model gives rise to concepts.
Concepts give rise to understanding.
Understanding gives rise to reasoning.
Reasoning gives rise to intelligent behavior.

Therefore, Concept Emergence is the bridge between Reality and Intelligence.
